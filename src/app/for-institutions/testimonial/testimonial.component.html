UNIVERSITY LEADERS SHARE THEIR THOUGHTS

OneCampus® by Laureate encourages both faculty and students to question their perspectives about big issues by interacting with groups of students from other cultures.

It's an easy, quick and valuable way to internationalize a classroom. Having international students in the classrooms also raises the level of discourse across the board and gives the classroom a dynamic element that encourages deeper reflection.


Lulu Williamson, Ph.D
OneCampus® by Laureate Coordinator for Walden University
