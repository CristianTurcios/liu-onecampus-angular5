Two Ways to Engage

International Course Exchange

Joining OneCampus® by Laureate International Course Exchange is as easy as selecting one or more online courses from around the Laureate network to offer to your students.

You can also consider hosting your online courses with students around the network through OneCampus® by Laureate International Course Exchange! Showcase your exemplary online courses and allow students and faculty to experience the international perspective provided by their global classroom.

Student Destination

Offer the OneCampus® by Laureate website as your student destination to access online courses and connect your students with others from around the Laureate network. New features and resources are frequently added to address the needs of our 65+ institutions.
